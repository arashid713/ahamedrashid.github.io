The Future of Markets

  • The project uses quantitative analysis to forecast the movement of the SP500 stock market over the next 6 months.
  • The model uses a 52-week moving average to predict the price movement of the SP500.
  • The model predicts that the SP500 will remain stagnant over the next 6 months, with an average price of $301 in July, $302 in August, $303 in September, and $299 by January 1st.
  • The model also predicts that gold prices will continue to increase over the next 6 months, with a 10% error.
  • The project also analyzes the ISM Manufacturing Report on Business and the Fed's actions to increase liquidity in the economy.
  • The project concludes that the Fed's actions to increase liquidity will be the most important influence on the market over the next 6 months.
